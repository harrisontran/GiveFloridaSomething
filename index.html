Give Florida Something

Whenever the GFS sends a storm towards Florida...

The beloved Global Forecast System, our beloved Florida wishcast model, is a weather forecast model designed by the National Centers for Environmental Prediction. For unknown reasons, it has a tendency to send tropical and subtropical cyclones towards Florida, and many of these forecasts do not end up verifying. This is only a list of storms the GFS predicts will hit or impact Florida; there's a plethora of "phantom storms" that the GFS creates (e.g.), but those are not listed here. This page also only lists GFS deterministic or GEFS control runs; individual ensemble hype is not included.
